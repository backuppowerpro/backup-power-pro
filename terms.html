1. Services Provided

Backup Power Pro installs generator inlet boxes and interlock kits that allow portable generators to connect safely to a home's electrical panel. Our service includes:

  • Exterior generator inlet box (30A or 50A) mounted to the home
  • Interlock kit installed on the existing electrical panel
  • Wiring run from the panel to the inlet
  • Permit filing with the applicable county authority
  • Final inspection scheduled and completed

We do not sell, supply, or install portable or standby generators. We do not perform general electrical work outside the scope of generator inlet installation under this brand. Any work outside the agreed scope will be quoted and authorized separately before proceeding.
