Hey Sarah — the storms in Upstate SC ramp up over the next 4 months. Forecasters are calling for an above-average season this year.

The fix is simple: run your generator for 10 minutes this weekend, with the inlet hooked up, on actual house load. Any small issue surfaces now — not at 3 a.m. when the power's already out.

Your 10-minute test

01 Roll the generator out, fuel it.
Check oil while you're there. Top off if it's low.
02 Plug the yellow cord into the inlet, start the generator.
Let it run 60 seconds at idle to warm up.
03 Flip your main breaker OFF, then the generator breaker ON.
The interlock plate enforces the sequence — that's by design.
04 Run the fridge + a window AC + the kitchen lights for 5 minutes.
If anything sounds off, the generator bogs, or a breaker trips — text me before the season ramps.
