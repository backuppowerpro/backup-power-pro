## Shared visual tokens

- **SMART CHIP** — small (fontSize 9-10), high-contrast bg, uppercase label, consistent across surfaces. Already used in Smart List reason chips.
- **SMART RING** — 2px outline in tone color (gold/red) around a card or row to flag status.
- **SMART BAR** — slim horizontal strip (4-6px tall) above a section tinted by the signal (red=urgent, gold=peak-interest, green=good).
- **SMART HINT CARD** — small dismissible card at top of surface for "Next best action" style prompts. Navy bg + gold text.
- **SMART DOT** — 6px colored dot as a pre-content indicator (gold=hot, red=overdue, green=paid).

Tones:
- **gold** = peak interest, needs action
- **red** = overdue, stuck, urgent
- **green** = good, on track, paid
- **navy** = informational, neutral
- **purple** = permit / process
- **muted** = default / low priority
