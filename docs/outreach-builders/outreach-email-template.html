Builder & Developer Outreach

Email & Phone Templates

Three templates covering cold outreach, follow-up, and a phone script for when you reach someone live. Fill in the highlighted fields. Tone is contractor-to-contractor — direct, no fluff.

How to Use This Package

  1. Cold email: Use Template 1. Target: sales managers, project managers, or owners at production builders and custom home builders in Greenville/Pickens/Spartanburg. Find contacts on the builder's website or LinkedIn.
  2. Follow-up with spec sheet: Use Template 2 after a conversation. Attach builder-spec-sheet.html (print to PDF first).
  3. Phone script: Use Template 3 when you call in cold or when they pick up after an email. Keep it under 60 seconds — just get a meeting.

Template 3 — Phone Script (Cold Call or Warm Follow-Up)

Phone — keep it under 60 seconds
When they answer: "Hi, is this [Name]? [Yes.] Hey [Name], this is Key Goodson — I'm a local electrical contractor, I run a business called Backup Power Pro. Do you have about 60 seconds?" [If yes, continue. If bad time, ask: "What's a better time to call back?"] "We install generator inlet connections — the outlet on the outside of a home that lets a portable generator power the panel directly. After the storms we've had, buyers are starting to ask about this, and I wanted to reach out because I think it could be a good add-on for your homes. The whole thing is one day, fully permitted, starts at twelve hundred bucks. You'd refer the buyer and we handle everything from there. Would it make sense to set up 15 minutes so I can show you exactly what it looks like?" [If they ask about pricing or process:] "I've got a one-page spec sheet that covers all of it — if I send it over today, would you have a chance to look at it this week?" [If they say not interested:] "No problem. Is it a timing thing, or just not something that fits your buyers right now?" [listen — don't push] [To close the call:] "I'll shoot you a quick email so you have my info. Thanks for your time — I hope we can do something together."
Notes: The key phrase is "buyers are starting to ask about this." Builder sales people respond to buyer demand signals. Don't lead with the product — lead with the market signal. If they bite, get a meeting or permission to send the spec sheet. That's the only goal of the call.
