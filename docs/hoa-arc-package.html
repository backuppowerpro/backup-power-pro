Wire runFrom panel to inlet — runs inside wall or surface conduit
BreakerDedicated generator breaker added to panel
Backfeed protectionInterlock kit physically prevents backfeed — utility workers are protected

Code Compliance Statement

This installation is performed in full compliance with the National Electrical Code (NEC) and applicable South Carolina state electrical standards. A building/electrical permit is filed with the county authority having jurisdiction (AHJ) on every job — this is not optional and is included in the project price.

A county electrical inspector performs a final inspection after installation. The interlock kit is a UL-listed device that physically prevents the generator and utility power from being connected to the panel simultaneously, eliminating the risk of backfeeding the utility grid (required by NEC Article 702).

Permitted work means the installation is documented in the public record and compliant with all local building codes.
