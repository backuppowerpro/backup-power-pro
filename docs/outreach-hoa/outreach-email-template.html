HOA & Neighborhood Board Outreach

Email & Message Templates

Three templates for different stages of HOA/neighborhood outreach. Use these word for word or adapt them — the tone is intentionally plain and direct, not salesy. Fill in the highlighted fields before sending.

How to Use This Package

  1. First contact (cold): Use Template 1 to reach the board president or property manager. Goal is to get a 10-minute conversation, not to sell anything.
  2. Follow-up / send docs: Use Template 2 after a conversation, when they ask for more info. Attach neighborhood-pitch.html (print to PDF first).
  3. Homeowner forward: Use Template 3 — this is written for the board to forward to their own homeowners once they've agreed to share it.
