## YOUR BPP-SPECIFIC ADVANTAGES (Why You'll Hit 20 Faster Than Most)

1. **Pre-qualified buyers already exist** — Every homeowner who owns a portable generator is your customer. They already paid $800–$2,000 for a machine they can't fully use. You're not selling them something new — you're unlocking what they already own.

2. **The sunk cost hook is the strongest in home services** — "You paid $1,500 for that generator. It's sitting in your garage right now doing nothing." This closes faster than any other home service angle.

3. **$1,400 vs $15,000 price anchor** — No other home service has this kind of comparison. Standby generator = $15K installed. BPP = $1,600. That's not a sale, that's a rescue.

4. **Storm market** — Upstate SC gets hurricanes, ice storms, tornadoes. Every storm warning is a free marketing event. Your future customers sit in the dark for 3 days, then Google you. No ad spend needed.

5. **HOA multiplier** — One email to an HOA manager reaches 50–300 homeowners who already know and trust each other. When one gets the install and talks at the pool, 3 more call you.

6. **You already have the list** — 29 HOA managers + 48 builders = 77 relationships that cost $0 to start but could deliver 5–10 jobs/month on autopilot once active.

Most home service businesses fight for new customers every month. BPP has a built-in compound growth system:

**Every job generates:**
- 1 yard sign → 20+ neighbor impressions
- 1 Nextdoor post → 30–200 neighbor views
- 1 door hanger campaign → 150–200 neighbor touches
- 1 referral card → 25–40% chance of a referred job
- 1 Google review → permanently improves GBP ranking

**By month 6, your organic job flow looks like this:**
- 20 total installs done → 20 yard sign neighborhoods → 3,000–4,000 direct neighbor impressions
- 20 Nextdoor posts → 600–4,000 local views
- 20 referral cards given → 5–8 active referrals in pipeline
- 20 Google reviews → top 3 GBP in Greenville market

At that point, **you need to REDUCE ad spend** because organic demand starts to exceed capacity.
