This is the weakest dimension. Don gets no explicit confirmation that he's been removed. From Don's UX perspective:

- Turn 3: he sends "STOP" — still no confirmation. Was that a dead number? Did it work?

TCPA technically requires honoring opt-out requests but does NOT require sending a confirmation SMS. The bot is legally compliant. But from a consumer-experience standpoint, the standard expectation (set by every marketing SMS ever) is that you get a "You've been unsubscribed" reply.
