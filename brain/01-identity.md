## Why this business

> Captured from Key directly, 2026-05-05.

Other electrical work is fine, you get paid hourly and the job is the job. **This work is different: Key gets to help somebody regain their feeling of power during a crisis.** The phrase "feeling of power" is doing double duty here, literal (electricity flowing) and emotional (agency, control, not at the storm's mercy).

**Implications for everything BPP makes:**
- The product isn't an inlet box. The product is **the feeling of power restored during a crisis.** Copy that frames it as the latter outperforms copy that frames it as the former.
- The customer's emotional state at form-fill is "I don't want to feel powerless again." Meet them there.
- Anything that adds scope ambiguity (whole-home, ATS, commercial) violates the "defined and consistent" appeal that makes this work feel right to Key. That's another reason out-of-scope rejections are real, not just an arbitrary boundary.
