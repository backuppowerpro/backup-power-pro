Backup Power Pro
One brand, every surface.

The language shared by backuppowerpro.com, customer proposals, customer invoices, and the internal CRM. Navy + gold, Outfit display on Inter body, confident pill CTAs, soft shadows, generous radii. 02 · Typography

Outfit for voice, Inter for data

04 · Radii

4, 8, 16, 20, 28, pill

Six steps. 16 covers most cards. 20 is reserved for hero cards and modals. Every CTA, chip, and eyebrow badge is a pill (100px).

4px — tight controls
8px — buttons, inputs
16px — cards
20px — hero / modal
28px — large panels
Pill — CTAs, chips
05 · Buttons

Three pill tiers

One primary per screen (gold), one secondary (navy), everything else ghost. Icon-only pills for top-bar chrome.

06 · Hero card

The one navy moment per viewport

Navy fill, 20px radius, white body, gold accents. Proposal hero, CRM briefing, login chrome, and smart-hint callouts all use this. Never stack two in a row.

12 · Motion

Smooth ease-out, no stepped jumps

Default ease cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1). Durations 140 / 200 / 300ms. Hover lifts 1px, active presses back, never more than a 3% scale.
