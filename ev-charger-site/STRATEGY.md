# EV CHARGER CHOICE — HORMOZI-INFORMED GROWTH STRATEGY

## The $100M Leads Framework Applied

Hormozi says there are only 4 ways to get customers:
1. **Warm outreach** — reach out to people you know
2. **Cold outreach** — reach out to strangers
3. **Content** — create free value that attracts people to you
4. **Paid ads** — pay to put your offer in front of strangers

For an affiliate content site, #3 (Content) is the primary engine, supported by #2 (Cold outreach via Reddit/forums) and eventually #4 (Paid ads).

## Hormozi Principles Applied Throughout

### 1. Value Equation
**Dream Outcome** (find the perfect charger) × **Perceived Likelihood** (expert reviews, comparison tables) ÷ **Time Delay** (instant answers) ÷ **Effort & Sacrifice** (we did the research for you) = **VALUE**

### 2. Give Away the Secrets, Sell the Implementation
- We give away ALL the information for free (reviews, comparisons, guides)
- The "implementation" is clicking our affiliate link and buying — zero friction

### 3. Volume > Everything
- More articles = more keywords = more traffic = more revenue
- A mediocre article that exists beats a perfect article that doesn't
- Publish first, polish later

### 4. Lead with Giving
- Every Reddit post, every Quora answer, every Pinterest pin GIVES value first
- Never lead with "check out my site" — lead with the actual answer
- Trust earns clicks, clicks earn revenue

### 5. 100 Rule
- Post 100 pieces of content before evaluating whether this works
- Don't judge results at article 10. Judge at article 100.
- Most people quit at 15. That's why most people don't make money.

## Daily Agent Workflow (When Autonomous)

**Morning Session (4 hours):**
1. Read PROGRESS.md
2. Check Google Search Console for new impressions/clicks (when available)
3. Write 1 new article (highest priority from content calendar)
4. Create Pinterest pin for the article
5. Update sitemap.xml and PROGRESS.md

**Afternoon Session (2 hours):**
1. Find and respond to 3 Reddit threads with genuine value
2. Answer 1 Quora question
3. Update internal links across existing articles
4. Check and fix any broken links or formatting issues

**Weekly Review:**
1. Which articles are getting impressions? Write more on those topics.
2. Which articles are getting zero? Re-optimize titles and meta descriptions.
3. Email list growth rate — if stagnant, test new lead magnet angle.
4. Revenue per article — identify top performers and create more like them.
